Active Vacation

What to Do

Adventures at every turn

The Makarska Riviera is more than a place to rest — it's a playground for the curious and the active. From the crystal-clear Adriatic to the rugged peaks of the Biokovo mountains, every day holds a new adventure waiting just outside your pitch.

Swimming & Beach

Our private pebble beach sits at the foot of the campsite, lapped by the clear turquoise waters of the Adriatic. Spend lazy mornings swimming in calm, shallow bays, or claim a sunlounger and let the sea breeze do its work. The water is clean, warm from June through September, and perfect for snorkelling right from the shore.

Kayaking & Water Sports

Paddle along the coastline, discover hidden sea caves, and explore coves that are only reachable by water. Kayak and stand-up paddleboard rentals are available near the beach. For something faster, jet-ski and boat hire can be arranged locally. Whether you're a first-timer or an experienced paddler, the calm inshore waters make this a family favourite.

Cycling

Cycle along the coastal path with the sea on one side and fragrant pine forests on the other. Marked cycling routes connect the campsite to the nearby towns of Baška Voda and Brela. Bikes can be rented at the campsite or locally. For those who want more of a challenge, mountain bike trails lead up towards the lower slopes of Biokovo.

Hiking & Biokovo

The Biokovo Nature Park rises steeply behind the coast, offering dramatic hiking with unforgettable views over the Adriatic and the Dalmatian islands. Trails range from easy forest walks to demanding ridge hikes reaching over 1,700 m. Don't miss the Biokovo Skywalk — a glass-floored viewing platform perched above the coast — one of the most spectacular spots in Croatia.

Diving & Snorkelling

The Adriatic around Makarska is exceptionally clear and rich with marine life — sea urchins, octopus, colourful fish, and rocky reef formations await beneath the surface. Snorkelling is excellent directly from the beach. For those wanting to go deeper, a local dive school offers guided dives, equipment rental, and PADI courses for complete beginners through to advanced divers.

Beach & Court Sports

Stay active on land with beach volleyball on the shore, tennis courts just minutes away, and table tennis tables on site. Our animation team organises tournaments and group games throughout the season — a great way to meet fellow campers. There's always something going on for kids and adults alike, from morning stretching sessions to evening beach games.
